# ClipBlaze Product Overview

ClipBlaze is an AI-powered video editing SaaS that transforms long-form videos into viral short-form content for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Core Value Proposition
- Automatically detect engaging moments in long videos
- Generate ready-to-post clips with captions, effects, and smart cropping
- Export professional-quality shorts in under 2 minutes

## Key Features
- AI Highlight Detection: Finds the most engaging moments automatically
- Auto Captions & Emojis: Word-by-word animated subtitles with smart emoji placement
- Smart 9:16 Crop: Intelligent reframing that tracks speakers
- Audio Enhancement: Volume normalization and noise reduction
- Dynamic Effects: Auto zoom, jump cuts, and motion tracking
- One-Click Export: Direct upload or download

## Target Users
Content creators, marketers, and social media managers who need to repurpose long-form content into short-form viral clips efficiently.
